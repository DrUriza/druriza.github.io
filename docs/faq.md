# TradElatin VR1 FAQ

## What is TradElatin VR1?
TradElatin VR1 is a market intelligence platform designed to analyze financial data with signal processing, statistical methods, and artificial intelligence to support professional interpretation and decision workflows.

## Is it an automated trading bot?
No. TradElatin VR1 is positioned as an intelligence and analytics platform. It is designed to generate structured insights and context, not to guarantee outcomes or promote automated profit claims.

## What markets can it analyze?
The architecture is designed to support multiple financial market data sources, including spot and derivatives-style streams where data contracts are available.

## Does it require exchange connectivity?
Not necessarily. The platform can operate with live feeds when available, but development and testing workflows can be executed without direct exchange connections.

## Can it work with simulated APIs?
Yes. The roadmap includes an API Emulator to provide simulated market interfaces for testing, model validation, and operational rehearsal.

## What is the long-term vision?
The long-term vision is to build a scalable, explainable, and extensible intelligence platform that supports technical partnerships, startup growth, and future open-source collaboration.
